Welcome to dream saviour! Here is some help to get you started. The following is a list of available input commands. Practice messing around with them.

Finish: Say this when you are done editing the current field.

Switch: Say switch followed by one of the categories to the right to switch to editing that category.

Save: Save the current dream. Then say "new" or "quit".

New: Starts a new dream, asking you to save first if you have not.

Quit: Discards all information and exits dream input mode.


Remember to please be patient as you are learning to interact with dream-saviour. Try to speak with a clear voice and turn the gain up on your microphone so you do not have to speak too loudly. The more loudly and clearly you speak, the easier it will be to recognize what you are saying. Finally, this software has ONLY been tested on new versions of google chrome browser, so please use that if you experience any problems. You can email dreamsaviourinfo@gmail.com for help.
